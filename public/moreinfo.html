good bad / bad bad

What is this?

It's a simple website that lets you vote if a (famously) bad movie is a good bad movie (enjoyable, heartfelt) or bad bad (nasty, unenjoyable).

If you're thinking that this sounds a bit like the rating system from the podcast the Flophouse, you would be correct!
